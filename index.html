Helpful Destiny 2 Resources

Destiny Vault Analyzer/

Vault Analyzer is a complicated DIM search that helps expose items that you might want to consider deleting.

https://destinyitemmanager.com/en/

Item management app for Destiny, transfer items between characters, create loadouts, find optimal stat distributions.

https://www.light.gg/

Destiny 2 Database with possible rolls, full stats, 3D previews, god rolls, leaderboards, reviews, and more. Find D2 weapons, armor, quests, triumphs, vendors, and cosmetics with our detailed tools.

https://destinysets.com/

One giant checklist for every single item in the game. If you link it to your account if will tell you what you have and what you're missing.

https://trials.report/

Check out a specific guardian and their fireteams trials stats.

https://guardian.report/

Tons of other guardian specific stats.

https://raid.report/

See how many raid clears a guardian has for all of the various raids. As well as some leaderboards.

https://www.d2checklist.com/

Literally a giant checklist that tracks milestones, can also sync with your account.

https://destinytracker.com/

Keeps track of a ton of PVP stats and will let you view your own as well as others. They also do elo calculation for every player queried to try and gauge skill level, it's pretty accurate in my experience. Howerver, it does fluctuate wildly.

https://braytech.org/

Destiny 2 character progression, clans, triumphs, collections, and more. Braytech is a Destiny fan site that allows users to view and map checklists, track and view triumphs, inspect collectibles, and so much more.

http://www.ishtar-collective.net/

All of the Destiny lore readable on a website. Also tagged so that you can search for specific things. Really easy to fall into a rabbit hole here.
